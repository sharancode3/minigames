Game Portal is a modern web-based gaming platform designed to provide an immersive gaming experience with user authentication, progress tracking, and a sleek red/black aesthetic. Inspired by popular gaming platforms like Poki, we've created a space where gamers can enjoy multiple games while tracking their achievements and competing with others.

üéì How It Works

1. Sign Up: Create your account with a username, email, and password. Or continue as a guest if you prefer.

2. Browse Games: Explore our collection of games. Use the search feature to find specific titles.

3. Play & Progress: Click any game to start playing. Your scores are automatically saved after each session.

4. Track Stats: View your progress, best scores, and improvement over time.

5. Compete: Compare your scores with other players on the leaderboards.
